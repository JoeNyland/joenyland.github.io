gmail-slack-notifier Gem

I created this Ruby Gem to fire a notification into Slack when you get a new email in your Gmail inbox.

I use Slack every day at work I find that I'm using email less and less. I decided to write a little gem that runs in the background which polls my Gmail mailbox and pings a message in Slack when I get a new email.

This was a the first Ruby Gem that I released to RubyGems.org.
