About

My name is Joe Nyland and I live in Warrington, UK.

Work

I started my career back in 2009, when I joined the system support team at Kaleida in Trafford Park, Manchester. At Kaleida, I gained experience supporting and maintaining Windows desktops and servers for around 400 users. I was asked to take on an important project, which utilised my experience with Linux and allowed me to gain commercial experience supporting and maintaining Linux servers and also the Asterisk VOIP communications server.

In January 2013, I took the decision to move to UKFast, who were based in Manchester city centre. I was employed at UKFast as a Linux engineer, providing telephone and email support in a diverse and fast paced business. During my time at both Kaleida and UKFast, I began to gain an interest in web sites and web development and in my spare time, I like to read and learn about web development as much as possible, to try and further my knowledge. I also enjoy keeping up to date on the latest and greatest open source projects.

You can find more information on my current projects in my portfolio.
